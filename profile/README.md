## About

Blackout Agency is a DJ booking, event production and music consultancy based in Singapore, curating and running dance-music experiences across Southeast Asia — from club nights to multi-day festivals.

## Flagship event — KAJE Festival 2026

[**KAJE Festival**](https://kajeartsandmusic.com) is Blackout Agency's flagship production: an intimate two-day highland music escape ~950 metres above sea level at Bagus Agro Pelaga, Bali. Two stages run sunset to sunrise across Disco, House, Funk, Ambient and Techno. 25–26 September 2026.

Tickets are sold exclusively through the KAJE App, instant e-tickets with account-bound live QR entry.

## Ticketing & payments — powered by CHI

Every Blackout Agency event runs on **[CHI](https://chi.app)** — our preferred whitelabel ticketing and payments platform. CHI powers our checkout, card/QR/token payments, and real-time door scanning under our own branded app, so we can focus on the music and the room.
